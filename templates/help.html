Help & Support

Registering Patients

To register a new patient, go to the Patients section and click Add Patient. Fill in the required demographic details. Medical data can be added later via the Add Medical Data option.

Running Predictions

Once medical data is entered, click Predict on the patient’s record. The system will run both Random Forest and SVM models, then display a consensus result.

Reports

Reports can be generated after predictions. You can view, export to PDF, or print reports directly from the Reports section.

Authentication & Security

Doctors and admins must log in with their credentials. Passwords can be changed under Change Password. Always log out after use to protect patient data.

Need More Help?

If you encounter issues, please contact the system administrator or IT support team for assistance.
